Hurricane Season 2021

October 1, 2021 by Jevon Forrest


Hurricane Sam is nearing Category 5 strength as it moves NNW over open waters. The latest reconnaissance pass into the storm captured 150kt flight level winds. That would indicate 150-155mph intensity which us just shy of Category 5 Strength. NHC will likely update Sam to a 155mph, 937mb Category 4 storm in its next advisory.
